What is Empower?

Empower is a site that allows you to log your emotional state every day. You can also choose to add a journal entry which allows you to expand on why you have chosen your selected emoji.

About Us

Why should I use Empower?

Our emotional health is a very important part of our overall health. By becoming more aware of our emotions can help us talk about our feelings, avoid or resolve conflicts in our lives better and help us to move past difficult feelings.

How to use Impower?

Empower allows you to take a moment out of each day to check in and log your emotions. You can then track your emotional health over time using the calendar function in your profile. The calendar may provide your emotional health history if you seek help from professionals.

Can anyone else see my information?

No. Your privacy is essential to all of us. You will need to register with the Empower site to log your emotional health and add notes. The password to your account will never be revealed to anybody, even to us. This process allows you to keep all your information confidential.
